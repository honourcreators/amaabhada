About Us

TASHUKERU is built upon the statement Vocal_for_local. We at TASHUKERU focus on amplifying our ODIA Culture & Heritage. Our aim is to give our customers, the ultimate happiness and satisfaction of being a part of growth for the small businesses & local vendors.

As per the co-founders Ashutosh Patra and Ashutosh Sahoo, the society can be a better place if people having a positive mindset come together to bring about a positive change in the lives of the less privileged people.

TASHUKERU is a Japanese word, in omitting 'H' will be referred to as Helping.

We are the bridge between small businesses and you. As an act of kindness never goes out of style, just like that your small contribution like placing an order here goes a long way in the long run.
